Virtual Tour Instructions

  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection as the virtual tour can take up to 2 minutes to load.
  • Click the "Start Virtual Tour" button below.
  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move.
  • Use the mouse to look around.
  • Use the V key to switch camera.
  • Refer to the map for your location.
  • Make sure your speakers are on to hear the audio.
    • Tip: There are three locations highlighted with purple columns.
    Move towards them:
    • The first two are on the road, the road is guided with invisible borders.
    • The third location is on the mountain, accessible after you cross the bridge.
